About Me

"Fun is good." - Dr. Seuss

I was born and raised in Miami, Florida. I was the only child of my Cuban American parents... for about 4 years. Then I became the older sibling when I got a younger brother. I stayed in Miami until 2012 when I left to go to the University of Florida in Gainesville, Florida. The only thing that I like more than a good Florida Gators football game is a good Miami Dolphins football game. Unfortunately both of those are hard to come by at the moment.

I love to run. I started to run in 2011 and since then I have tried to keep a consistent running routine. I have run a number of 5ks and 10ks, 3 half marathons and a full marathon. I began to run, like most other people do, to try to stay in shape. What I found though is a sport unlike any I have ever played before, a sport of pure willpower. To excel at running doesn't require wealth, being a 7 foot tall behemoth, or knowing anybody else who does it. All that it requires is the willpower to continue to move when the body desperately sends signals saying to stop. A good pair of running shos won't hurt though.

I also like to play games. In addition to board games like Chess, Settlers of Catan, and Ticket to Ride, I also like to play less traditional games like Mafia and Humans vs Zombies. I also enjoy videogames of just about any genre. Some of my favorites include The Last of Us, Bioshock Infinite, Portal 2, Hearthstone, Starcraft 2, Super Mario 3D World, Super Smash Bros for Wii U, Civilization 5, Banjo Kazooie, and Braid. That only begins to scratch the surface though.
